Game Rules

  1. The game is played on a 4√ó4 grid.
  2. Two players take turns (you can play vs AI or another player).
  3. On your turn, select one or more adjacent cells in the same row or column.
  4. Selected cells must form an unbroken line (no gaps between them).
  5. The player who removes the last remaining cell(s) wins the game.
